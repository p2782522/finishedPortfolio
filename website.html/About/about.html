Hello there! I'm Josh Chester, a photo and video editor
and Graphic Designer based in Leicester with a
passion to upgrade your photos and videos to the next level!

Originally from Northamptonshire, I am a Media Production
student that discovered my love for editing the
moment I created my first fan poster.

I primarily use the Adobe Creative Cloud, specifically
Photoshop, Premiere, Audition and After Effects, always
exploring new ways to enhance my work.

My work has always gone hand in hand with my hobbies:
waching movies and going out with friends! Movies and going
out always inspire me to create new pieces
of digital art as well as enhance personal moments
I capture with my friends.

Any assets that have to be purchased that you wish to
be used within your product will be included in the invoice.

All invoices come with a full breakdown of costs.
